Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining Peregrine.

Do I need previous rowing experience?

Not at all! We welcome complete beginners through our Learn To Row programme. Many of our most enthusiastic members had never rowed before joining Peregrine.

What fitness level do I need?

You should have a reasonable base level of fitness, but you don't need to be an athlete. Rowing will quickly build your strength and endurance. We have members of all fitness levels.

Do I need to be able to swim?

Yes, all members must be able to swim 50 metres in light clothing and tread water. This is a British Rowing safety requirement. A capsize drill is part of your induction.

What equipment do I need?

To start, just wear comfortable sports clothing that you don't mind getting wet. Leggings and a fitted top work well. The club provides all rowing equipment. You may want to invest in club kit later.

What's the time commitment?

We recommend attending at least one session per week to maintain technique. Racing squad members typically train 2-3 times per week. Sessions last about 2 hours including rigging and de-rigging.

Can I try before I commit?

We occasionally hold taster sessions. The best way to try rowing is through our annual Learn To Row programme. Get in touch and we'll let you know about upcoming opportunities.

Safety First

Your safety on the water is our top priority.

British Rowing Affiliated

We follow all British Rowing safety guidelines and best practices. All members receive comprehensive safety training.

Capsize Drill

All new members complete a capsize drill to ensure confidence in the water. You must swim 50m in light clothing.

Qualified Coaches

Our coaches hold British Rowing qualifications and first aid certifications. Safety boats available for all outings.
